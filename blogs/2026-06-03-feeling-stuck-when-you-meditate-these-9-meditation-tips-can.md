When your mind wanders during meditation, whether through grocery lists, old memories, or worry, this is actually a normal part of practice. Rather than indicating failure, the wandering is part of the work. The article provides nine practical strategies for common meditation challenges, including using physical anchors, adjusting your gaze, and accepting that a busy mind is natural.
