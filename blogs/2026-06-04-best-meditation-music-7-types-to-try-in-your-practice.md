Many people find meditating in silence difficult and distracting. This article explores how music can make meditation more effective by helping with stress relief, anxiety management, and sleep. The piece describes seven types of meditation music including ambient soundscapes, 432 Hz tuning, binaural beats, and nature sounds, with guidance on when to use each type based on your meditation goals.
